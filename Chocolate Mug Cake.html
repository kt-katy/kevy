Chocolate Mug Cake

Ingredients you would need

  • 4 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil (or melted butter)
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • 2-3 tbsp chocolate chips (optional)

What you would need

  • 1 microwave-safe mug (about 8 oz size)
  • 1 tablespoon and teaspoon (for measuring)
  • Fork or small whisk (to mix)
  • Microwave

Instructions:

  • Mix Dry Ingredients: In your microwave-safe mug, add 4 tbsp flour, 2 tbsp sugar, 2 tbsp cocoa powder, ¼ tsp baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Stir well with a fork or whisk until combined.
  • Add Wet Ingredients: Add 3 tbsp milk, 2 tbsp vegetable oil (or melted butter), and ½ tsp vanilla extract (optional). Stir the mixture with the fork until smooth and well combined.
  • Add Chocolate Chips (Optional): Stir in 2-3 tbsp chocolate chips or place them on top for a gooey topping.
  • Microwave the Mug Cake: Microwave the mug on high for 1 minute and 10-20 seconds. The cake should rise and set with a slightly soft top. If you want a firmer texture, microwave for an additional 10-15 seconds.
  • Cool and Serve: Let the cake cool for a minute or two. Optionally, top with whipped cream, ice cream, or a drizzle of chocolate syrup.
